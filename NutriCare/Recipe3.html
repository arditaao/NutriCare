Braised Beef with Chinese Five Spice Pomegranate

Ingredients You'll Need

  • Beef cuts: Short ribs, oxtails, stew meat, or neck bones. Adjust cooking times based on the cut.
  • Aromatic vegetables: Onion, carrots, celery, garlic cloves, and fresh ginger.
  • Dried spices: Peppercorns, green cardamom pods, star anise, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and bay leaves.
  • Pomegranate juice: For deglazing and adding to the pomegranate glaze.

Additionally, use beef broth, oyster sauce, and white rice for serving.

How To Make Braised Beef Without Wine

Mastering the braising process will change how you cook beef. Here’s how:

  1. Pat meat dry and season generously with salt.
  2. Heat oil in a Dutch oven and brown beef on all sides.
  3. Transfer beef to a plate, sauté onions, carrots, and celery until browned. Add garlic and ginger.
  4. Deglaze with pomegranate juice, scraping the pan.
  5. Add beef broth, spices, and browned beef back to the pot.
  6. Cover and cook for at least two hours, checking tenderness.

The process is simple and worth the wait. Most of the work is done upfront.
