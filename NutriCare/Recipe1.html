Skirt Steak Salad with Miso-Ginger Dressing

Ingredients You'll Need

Let’s start with the obvious: a skirt steak!

  • Selection Tips: Look for “inside skirt steak” or “skirt steak” at the meat counter.
  • What to Look For: A piece that’s at least 1 to 1 1/4 lbs, long and flat with marbling.
  • Seasoning Tips: Minimal seasoning like salt, black pepper, or your favorite steak seasoning.

Additional Ingredients:

  • Preshredded or chopped veggies: Broccoli slaw, shredded cabbage, or matchstick carrots.
  • Red bell pepper and green onions: Adds bright colors and flavors.
  • Crunchy garnishes: Toasted sesame seeds, crispy wonton strips, and roasted peanuts.
  • Dressing: Miso, grated ginger, neutral oil, toasted sesame oil, rice vinegar, and garlic.

How To Make Skirt Steak Salad

Lightly season the steak and heat your grill pan or cast iron skillet. Cook the steak for 2 1/2 to 3 minutes per side, then transfer to a cutting board and let it rest for at least 5 minutes before slicing.

Prepare the salad ingredients while the steak rests. For the dressing, combine all ingredients in a jar and shake well, or whisk together in a small bowl.

Slice the red bell pepper and green onions. Add them to a large mixing bowl with the broccoli slaw, shredded cabbage, and shredded carrots. Toss everything together until well mixed.
